NVES IS CHANGING THE GAME

The New Vehicle Efficiency Standard is adding real cost pressure to traditional ICE vehicles — and that's only going one direction. Novated leasing is an existing lever your customers already understand, used in a new way. It softens the sticker shock of NVES-impacted pricing, makes EVs a no-brainer with FBT exemption, and keeps ICE vehicles competitive through salary packaging. The timing has never been better to turn novated leasing into a core part of your sales process.

TEST, ROLL, COMPOUND

Our platform makes traditional ICE leasing just as competitive — so once the pipeline is running on EVs, you expand into utes, SUVs, and commercial vehicles. Each brand, each location multiplies the compounding effect exponentially.
